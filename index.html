About Me,

I get obsessed with making things run on their own.

Not automation for automation's sake but the kind where you build it once, it handles 50,000 records a day, and nothing quietly breaks at 2am. That reliability obsession is what drives everything I build.

I've always been drawn to the seam between systems where an API meets a database, where an AI agent needs to make a decision, where messy human input needs to become clean structured data. That's where I live

I started building full-stack apps to understand how products work end to end. Then I discovered that the most interesting problems weren't in the UI they were in the plumbing. The workflows nobody sees. The logic that runs whether or not anyone's watching.

That's what I build now. Systems with no silent failures.
